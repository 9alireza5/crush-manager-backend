# 💘 Crush Manager

**When your heart commits more than your code.**

## ❓ About

Crush Manager is a playful yet powerful web app that helps you keep track of your romantic ventures – the good, the bad, and the unforgettable.  

Think of it as your emotional CRM: log crushes, rate them, analyze patterns, and maybe, just maybe... avoid the next heartbreak.
